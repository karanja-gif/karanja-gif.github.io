The Clinician Who Became a Defender

I am John Mwaura Karanja — a Clinical Officer turned Cybersecurity Professional, currently pivoting full-force into a Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst role. Based in Mombasa, Kenya, I hold a Cybersecurity Program certificate from Mediacrest Training College (Class of 2026) covering Digital Forensics, Network Security, Threat Detection, Security Compliance, and Incident Management.

My foundation is not conventional — over 6 years as a Clinical Officer and Centre Manager taught me high-pressure decision making, systematic documentation, protocol adherence, and operational management under fire. At Bliss Medical Centre – Mtwapa, I led a team of 8+, served ~30 patients daily, and grew facility revenue by approximately 300% in 6 months — ranking Top 3 across 60+ branches.

These exact skills — triage, escalation, incident reporting, working under pressure — are what make a great SOC Analyst. I have simply moved from triaging patients to triaging cyber threats.
